# KG ElectroPower - Inverter Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues & Solutions

### ISSUE: Continuous Beeping (Red Light Only)
- **Diagnosis**: Battery level is critically low or there is an overload.
- **Troubleshooting Steps**:
  1. Check if heavy appliances like refrigerators or air conditioners are running on the inverter.
  2. Turn off all heavy loads immediately.
  3. Check the battery water level. 
  4. If beeping continues, reset the inverter using the main power switch at the back.

### ISSUE: Inverter Not Turning On
- **Diagnosis**: Blown fuse or loose battery connection.
- **Troubleshooting Steps**:
  1. Check the main fuse at the back of the unit.
  2. Ensure the DC cables are tightly connected to the battery terminals.
  3. Check if the battery is completely discharged.

### ISSUE: Low Backup Time
- **Diagnosis**: Battery aging or sulphation.
- **Troubleshooting Steps**:
  1. Check the specific gravity of the battery acid.
  2. Increase the charging current setting if supported.
  3. Clean the battery terminals to remove carbon/lead-oxide buildup.
