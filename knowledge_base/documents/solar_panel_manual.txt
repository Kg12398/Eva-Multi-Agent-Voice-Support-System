# KG ElectroPower - Solar Panel Maintenance & Safety

## Performance Management

### ISSUE: Low Power Generation (Sunny Day)
- **Diagnosis**: Shading, dust accumulation, or micro-cracks.
- **Troubleshooting Steps**:
  1. Inspect the panel surface for bird droppings or thick dust.
  2. Clean the panels with plain water and a soft micro-fiber cloth (Early morning only).
  3. Ensure no new trees or structures are casting a shadow on the array.

### ISSUE: Panel Discoloration (Hotspots)
- **Diagnosis**: Bypass diode failure or cell damage.
- **Troubleshooting Steps**:
  1. Do NOT touch the panel as it may be extremely hot.
  2. Disconnect the panel from the string if output is zero.
  3. Schedule a professional thermal imaging inspection.
